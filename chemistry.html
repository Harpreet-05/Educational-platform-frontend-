About the Book

"Introduction to Chemistry" by Nivaldo J. Tro is a widely acclaimed book designed for beginners. It provides a clear and thorough introduction to the principles of chemistry, balancing theoretical concepts with practical examples.

About the Author

Nivaldo J. Tro is a Professor of Chemistry known for his clear and engaging teaching style. His books are renowned for their real-world applications and their ability to simplify complex topics for students.

Key Topics Covered

  • Atomic Structure and Periodic Table
  • Chemical Bonding and Reactions
  • Stoichiometry and Mole Concept
  • Acids, Bases, and Solutions
  • Thermodynamics and Kinetics
